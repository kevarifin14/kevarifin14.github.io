# Inspiration Catalog — interactive simulation & explorable sites

Study these for *what good feels like*, then steal the interaction, not the code.

## Contents
- Gold standard (interactive articles)
- Physics & science simulations
- Math / CS visual explanations
- 3D / WebGL
- Generative & shaders
- Fun / viral interactive
- Curated lists

## Gold standard (interactive articles)
- **Bartosz Ciechanowski** — https://ciechanow.ski/ — the benchmark; every diagram is a draggable real-time sim ([Mechanical Watch](https://ciechanow.ski/mechanical-watch/), [GPS](https://ciechanow.ski/gps/), [Gears](https://ciechanow.ski/gears/)). Hand-coded canvas/WebGL, no frameworks. **Steal:** scroll-linked sims that each isolate one idea.
- **Nicky Case** — https://ncase.me/ — playful guided explorables ([Trust](https://ncase.me/trust/), [Polygons](https://ncase.me/polygons/), [Loopy](https://ncase.me/loopy/)). **Steal:** gentle guided narrative around the toy.

## Physics & science simulations
- **Paul Falstad** — https://www.falstad.com/mathphysics.html — canonical applets; the [Ripple Tank](https://www.falstad.com/ripple/) and [Circuit Sim](https://www.falstad.com/circuit/) are the references. **Steal:** sources/walls/presets UI for field sims.
- **PhET (CU Boulder)** — https://phet.colorado.edu/ — largest research-backed sim library. **Steal:** approachable defaults, teacherly framing.
- **myPhysicsLab** — https://www.myphysicslab.com/ — real-time ODE physics (pendulums, springs) showing math↔motion. **Steal:** fixed-timestep integrators.
- **Complexity Explorables** — https://www.complexity-explorables.org/ — chaotic/complex systems, beautifully done.
- **Pink Trombone** — https://dood.al/pinktrombone/ — a simulation as a toy; cult classic.

## Math / CS visual explanations
- **Red Blob Games** — https://www.redblobgames.com/ — best-in-class interactive algorithm guides (A*, hex grids). **Steal:** drag-to-understand explanations.
- **Setosa / Explained Visually** — https://setosa.io/ — eigenvectors, Markov chains.
- **Seeing Theory** — https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/ — probability & stats, animated.
- **Mathigon** — https://mathigon.org/ — full interactive textbook.
- **TensorFlow Playground** — https://playground.tensorflow.org/ — tweak a neural net live.
- **A Primer on Bézier Curves** — https://pomax.github.io/bezierinfo/ · **Circles, Sines and Signals** — https://jackschaedler.github.io/circles-sines-signals/

## 3D / WebGL

- **three.js examples** — https://threejs.org/examples/ · **Awwwards three.js** — https://www.awwwards.com/websites/three-js/
