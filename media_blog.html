Media Reflections

As a 2016 New Year's resoultion, my friend Mitas and I are planning on writing short reflections on every movie and book we watch and read this year. Every media experience is shaped by the context of our current perspectives on life. I feel like movies and books are such important experiences in our lives, giving us more insight into life and ourselves. My hope is that this New Year's resolution will help me remember the impact that different media has had on me throughout my life.
